# Axes of variation

For each challenge, the 3–4 groups working on it identify the *dimensions* along which their apps could meaningfully differ. The output is a **map**, not a position on the map — groups commit to specific positions in the async homework after Session 1.

## Why "axes" instead of "differentiators"

Differentiation needs a coordinate system. Asking groups to differentiate without a shared map produces vague, mismatched answers ("we're more user-centric"; "we're more data-driven"). Asking them to first agree on the map — *what dimensions could apps in this challenge differ along?* — gives each group a real position to stake out.
